Introduction


AI Flappy Bird is a game played by machine brain which is based on neural networks. You can choose 2 different modes.



AI Mode:

This mode lets AI play the Flappy Bird game. By hitting "Start AI", AI starts learning to find an optimal solution to archive as high score as possible. The AI repeat the game until you stop it. You can also see what AI looks at by turning on "Analysis".



Player Mode:

You can also play the Flappy Bird game by yourself. Start the game by hitting SPACE or TAP the screen if it's a smartphone.
